History

The Psychology Club at Glynn Academy has a long history among its several iterations. Long overseen by Coach Knowles, who has taught at Glynn Academy since 2005Wow!, the club has historically conducted business out of the media center, until its untimely expiration in 2019, when its last populated cadre graduated. Three, going on four years later, a handful of students in Coach Knowles AP Psychology course became interested in the derelict club. Ginger West, our Social Director, had in fact been a member of its prior iteration during her freshman year. Looking to reconvene the club, she brought together a handful of like-minded individuals to work with. Rather than returning entirely to form, the four decided to form a directorate, wherein all four would share administrative power over sections of club work. Our directors hope that under this new format, the club will be better able to integrate both with on-campus and local involvement efforts.
